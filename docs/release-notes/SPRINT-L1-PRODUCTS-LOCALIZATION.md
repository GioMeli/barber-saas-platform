# Sprint L1 — Products Localization

## Scope

Localized the owner Products & Inventory module in English, Greek, German, Spanish and Turkish.

## Included

- Page header, metrics, tabs, filters and responsive product views
- Product create/edit dialog and validation
- Stock adjustment workflow and movement types
- Stock history, alerts, statuses, empty/loading states and action labels
- Toasts, confirmation text, tooltips and accessibility labels
- Locale-aware inventory movement dates

## Safety

No database schema, Supabase query, stock calculation or inventory business rule was changed.
