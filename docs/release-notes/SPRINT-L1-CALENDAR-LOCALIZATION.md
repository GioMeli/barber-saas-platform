# Sprint L1 — Calendar Localization

## Scope

Localized the owner calendar and appointment workflows across English, Greek, German, Spanish, and Turkish.

## Included

- FullCalendar toolbar, views, staff filters, loading state, overflow labels, and locale-aware calendar headings.
- New appointment wizard, customer creation, service selection, professional selection, availability, review, and booking summary.
- Appointment detail dialog, status badges, contact details, and status actions.
- Staff delay preview and apply flow.
- Calendar toasts, validation, confirmations, errors, and success messages.
- Locale-aware appointment dates and moved-appointment messages.
